# Underground Music Platform Business Plan

This directory contains the detailed business plan, projections, and operational guidelines for securing funding and launching the platform in India.

## Contents

- [Business Plan Master Document](./Business_Plan_Master.md): The main, extremely detailed comprehensive business plan including timelines, P&L, budget, constraints, operations, and compliance needs.
- [Investor Pitch Deck Blueprint](./03_Pitch_Deck_Slide_by_Slide.md): An aggressively detailed, slide-by-slide structure for your PowerPoint pitch deck (includes visual cues and comprehensive speaker notes).

### Key Highlights inside the Master Plan:
1. **Executive Summary**: Funding Ask & Core Value Proposition.
2. **Legal Structure**: Best practices for incorporating a Private Limited tech startup in India, including Startup India and Trademark fees.
3. **Statutory Compliance**: Detailed breakdown of FEMA (Foreign Remittances), TDS (Withholding tax on international artists), DTAA requirements (TRC, Form 10F), and GST.
4. **Licensing**: Requirements for event execution (IPRS, PPL, NOCs).
5. **Team & HR**: Exact team compositions required to execute tech & ground-level event operations simultaneously.
6. **Financial Projections**: An 18-month projected budget (capital allocation mapping to a $500k ask) and Year 1 simulated P&L.
7. **Timelines**: Month-by-month execution runway.

> **Note**: This documentation is prepared based on current Indian operational constraints and is geared specifically for pitching to angel investors and VCs.
