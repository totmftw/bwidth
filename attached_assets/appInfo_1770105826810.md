## üîÑ Core Workflows & Modes

### **Mode A: Booking Mode**

**For:** One-off gigs or tour-based events

**Flow:**

1. **Curated Discovery**
    - Filter by budget range
    - Filter by genre/style
    - Filter by experience level
    - Filter by availability dates
    - Only show serious, verified options
2. **Intent Verification**
    - Required fields: event date, budget range, venue capacity
    - Payment structure commitment
    - Venue details mandatory
    - Prevents window shopping
3. **Structured Negotiation**
    - Limited to 2-3 rounds maximum
    - Pre-set negotiation parameters
    - Slot time, date (limited changes)
    - No open-ended WhatsApp chaos
    - Time-bound responses
4. **Auto Contract Generation**
    - Instant contract on acceptance
    - 24-48 hour window to sign
    - Auto-cancellation if not signed
    - All terms locked in
5. **Payment Commitment**
    - 20% deposit immediately on acceptance
    - Staged payment milestones
    - Penalty clauses for delays
    - Platform escrow protection
6. **Execution Checklist**
    - Travel arrangements uploaded
    - Accommodation confirmed
    - Technical rider acknowledged
    - Day-of logistics confirmed
    - All boxes checked before show day
7. **Event Completion & Feedback**
    - Dual confirmation (both parties)
    - Trust score updates
    - Internal feedback (no public drama)
    - Payment released

---

### **Mode B: Programming Mode**

**For:** Venues wanting 3-6 month calendars

**Flow:**

1. **Venue Intake**
    - Budget allocation per month
    - Audience definition and demographics
    - Music policy and genre preferences
    - Frequency (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly)
2. **Calendar Generation**
    - 3-6 month advance programming
    - Curator-led selections
    - Consistent quality and variety
    - Pattern-based bookings
3. **Artist Pipeline**
    - Pre-vetted artist pool
    - Rotation strategy
    - Mix of established and emerging
    - Genre diversity planning
4. **Monthly Retainers**
    - Fixed monthly fee for programming
    - Guaranteed artist supply
    - Consistent crowd building
    - Long-term relationship

**5. Technical Requirements**

- Equipment specifications
- Sound check time

**6. Hospitality**

- Guest list allocation (5-10 passes)
- Green room access
- Drinks and refreshments
- Meal arrangements

**7. Billing & Promotion**

- Artist name spelling
- Logo and artwork usage
- Promotional material approval
- Social media guidelines
- Press requirements
- Featured billing position

**8. Reproduction Rights**

- Recording permissions
- Photography/videography rules
- Live streaming rights
- Social media posting rules
- Content ownership

**9. Cancellation Policy**

- Organizer cancellation: no refund, penalties
- Artist cancellation: deposit forfeiture
- Force majeure clauses
- Notice periods (90 days for artist)
- Rescheduling options

**10. Slot Time Protection**

- Committed time slots cannot be changed
- If organizer changes: 50% to artist, 30% refund, 20% platform fee
- If artist accepts change: continues
- If artist denies: booking cancelled with compensation

- Confidentiality clause
- No partnership creation
- Liability limitations
- Dispute resolution
- Jurisdiction

- Four contract types for different scenarios
